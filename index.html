What is CarbonEye and how does it work?

Have you ever wondered how your daily choices impact the planet? With CarbonEye, understanding your carbon footprint has never been easier. Our app lets you calculate the carbon emissions regarding your lifestyle by simply inputting relevant information about your activities and consumption. But CarbonEye is more than just a calculator. Meet our AI assistant, EcoBot! EcoBot works to be your personal eco-assistant, and whether you're looking for practical tips to reduce your carbon footprint, seeking information about sustainable practices, or justs facts about global warming, our chatbot is here to help. It is accessible to all as it can communicate in multiple languages. Just ask, and you'll receive tailored advice and insights to guide you toward more eco-friendly choices. With CarbonEye, you’ll not only gain awareness of your environmental impact but also discover actionable steps you can take to live more sustainably. Together, let's make every choice count—because every action matters.

What is the issue we are trying to solve?

Our application is built to address the critical issue of climate change by encouraging induviduals to manage their carbon footprint by calculating the amount of CO2 emmissions they create in their everyday lives. Carbon dioxide is a primary greenhouse gas which is responsible for trapping heat in the Earth's atmosphere. Increase human activity haas been increasing the amount of CO2 emmissions, which significantly contributes to global warming. Global warming has many negative affects on our environment, including extreme weather events, melting ice caps, ocean acidification, biodiversity loss, forest threats, etc. Just by making simple changes in our lifestyle, we can slow down the rate of global warming.
