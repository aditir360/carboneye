Manual Footprint Tracker

1. Select Category: Start by choosing the category that best fits your item.

2. Enter Item Details: Provide specific information about the item you're calculating emissions for. Be as detailed as possible to ensure accurate results. Look at the two possible example below:
For transportation: Specify the type of vehicle, fuel type, and distance traveled.
For food: Indicate the type of food item and its weight or serving size.

3. View Results: After entering the item details, the app will calculate and display the estimated CO2 emissions associated with that item. It will also provide alternative products that are more environment friendly if the CO2 emissions calculated by your object are too high.

4. Get Sustainable Tips: If you have questions about reducing your carbon footprint or need sustainable tips, feel free to ask our AI assistant, EcoBot.
